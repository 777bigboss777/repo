The EmblemHealth Family of Companies


April 27, 2022

Over the last several years, we have all worked collectively to build an enterprise mindset that harnesses the individuality of each of our brands while leveraging the strength of our common mission: to create healthier futures for our customers, patients, and communities. Today, we are pleased to introduce the new brand architecture (a system that defines the relationship between a company’s parent or corporate brand and its subsidiaries, affiliates, sub-brands, products, or services) and logo that follows how we’ve been positioning ourselves internally as a family of companies.

The EmblemHealth family of companies is composed of EmblemHealth, the parent company, and its subsidiaries and affiliates, ConnectiCare, AdvantageCare Physicians, EmblemHealth Neighborhood Care, EmblemHealth Health@Work, EmblemHealth Family Dental, BronxDocs, and WellSpark Health. Together, we function as an enterprise, leveraging and distributing our tools and resources to enhance our business and improve our customers’ and patients’ experiences.

The EmblemHealth Family of Companies


Each brand within the EmblemHealth family of companies has been purposefully created with their own unique identity and logo. Therefore, it is important to continue to follow each brands’ guidelines and use the correct logos. When communicating with our members, patients, and partners across the EmblemHealth family of companies, our brand architecture functions behind the scenes, helping us create synergies and organize our messaging in a clear, consistent manner for our audience.


The EmblemHealth Family of Companies logo is reserved for limited internal use when communicating to or on behalf of the entire enterprise. Sample use cases: Internal communications & presentations; Industry thought leadership; EmblemHealth Family of Companies press.

Any external usage must be approved by the Chief Corporate Affairs Officer and the Brand team.
